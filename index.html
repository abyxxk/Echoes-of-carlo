Echoes of Carlo:
A Modern Saint for the Internet Age

Welcome to Carlos Era

Carlo Acutis, an Italian teenager born in 1991, used his love for the Eucharist and skill in technology to spread faith through documenting miracles, lived a joyful and simple life, offered his suffering from leukemia at age 15 for the Church and the Pope, and was beatified in 2020 as the “Patron of the Internet.”

Lets Volunteer With Carlo

Here are some of Carlo Acutis’s main activities during his short life:


  • Daily Mass & Eucharist – He never missed daily Mass and prayed the Rosary regularly.
  • Website on Miracles – He created a website cataloging Eucharistic miracles worldwide.
  • Helping Others – He often helped the poor, homeless, and classmates who were struggling.
  • Technology & Coding – He used his computer skills to spread the faith in a creative way.
  • School & Friends – He was cheerful, friendly, loved soccer, video games, and enjoyed spending time with friends.

Blessed Carlo Acutis (1991–2006)

  • 1991 – Born in London, later moved to Milan, Italy.
  • Childhood – Developed a deep love for Jesus, regularly prayed the Rosary, and attended daily Mass.
  • Early Teen Years – Learned computer programming and used his skills to build a website showcasing Eucharistic miracles.
  • Everyday Life – Helped the poor, defended bullied classmates, and shared joy with everyone around him.
  • 2006 – Diagnosed with leukemia at age 15; offered his suffering for the Pope and the Church.
  • 2006 (October 12) – Passed away peacefully, leaving behind a legacy of faith and love.

Canonization Ceremony

  • Date & Venue: On September 7, 2025, Carlo Acutis will be officially canonized alongside Blessed Pier Giorgio Frassati at St. Peter’s Square in Vatican City, officiated by Pope Leo XIV.
  • Significance: He will become the first millennial to be declared a saint—marking a meaningful moment for young Catholics worldwide.

Prayer To Blessed Carlos

O Blessed Carlo Acutis, you used your love for the Eucharist and your gift with technology to bring others closer to Jesus. Help us to live with the same purity, kindness, and joy that you showed. Guide us to use our talents for good, to care for the poor, and to grow in faith each day. Pray for us that we may always keep Jesus at the center of our lives, just as you did.
Blessed Carlo Acutis, pray for us!

Carlo Acutis: A Modern Role Model for Youth

Carlo Acutis influenced the youth by proving that even in today’s modern world a teenager can live a holy and joyful life; he showed that true happiness is found in Jesus and the Eucharist, used technology positively to spread faith, lived simply while still enjoying games and sports, helped the poor and stood up for others, inspired kindness among friends, and reminded young people everywhere that holiness is for everyone and that each person is created to be an “original” with a unique mission from God.

A Modern Teen, A True Spiritual Leader

Carlo Acutis was not a leader by title or position, but he became a true leader by example. Through his deep love for the Eucharist, his kindness toward others, his simple and joyful life, and his creative use of technology to spread faith, he inspired countless young people. Carlo showed that leadership is not about power or authority, but about guiding others toward truth and goodness through the way we live.

Shape Your Life With Carlos

Carlo Acutis reminds us that shaping our lives begins with choosing what truly matters. He showed that holiness is not far away or difficult, but lived daily through love, simplicity, and faith in God. Carlo shaped his own life by making the Eucharist his greatest treasure, by using his talents and technology to spread goodness, and by treating everyone with kindness and respect. His example challenges us to shape our lives not around temporary things, but around values that last forever—faith, love, and service. By following his path, we too can discover joy and purpose in every moment of life.
